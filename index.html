Better from the Source

Shopping for vegetables at the local farmer's market is fun, but getting them direct from a farm share is even better. From week to week, the selection changes. Some weeks you just don't know what to do with your crop. These mystery boxes encourage improvisation and learning, eating what's in season and interacting with the people responsible for producing the food.
